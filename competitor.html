COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

    Domestic Competitors: Within Cameroun, Coffee producers, and Cooperatives are key competitors. SOme notable competitors include Kawa Cooperative, Muongo Cooperative and COOPAC Cooperative.
    International Competitors: Cameroun's Coffee competes with products from other coffee-producing
    countries in Africa such as Ethiopia, Kenya and Ivory-Coast

    Domestic Competitors:In Cameroun, major tea producers and exporters such as Ndawara Tea, Kuku Tea Company and Mulin Japan. One are significant competitors. These companies focus on producing high quality orthodox and CTC teas for both domestic and International markets.
    International Competitors: Cameroun's tea competes with tea produced in other African countries like Kenya, Malawi, Tanzania which are known for their large scale tea plantations and established tea industries.

    Domestic Competitors:In Cameroun, horticulture producers and exporters such as Kribi LTD, Minagri Horticulture and CDC are key competitors.
    International Competitors: Cameroun's horticultural products compete with imports from other Africa countries such as Kenya, South Africa and Ethiopia which have well-established horticulture industries and export network Additionally, horticultural products from countries in Europe, Asia and South America pose competition in International markets due to factors such as lower production costs and established trade relationships.
